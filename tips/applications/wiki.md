### TIP-4.x
wiki
=====

`draft` `author:wds4`

## Synopsis

Curation of a wiki

## Data Model

## User Actions

Any user can:
- create a new wiki
- submit an article to any wiki
- submit a new category of articles
- endorse that an article belongs / does not belong to the wiki
- endorse any given user as a curator of articles for a given category
